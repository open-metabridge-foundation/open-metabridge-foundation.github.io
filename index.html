What is OMBF?

The OMBF aims to define and specify an interoperable and agnostic family of protocols that are based on linking/connecting physical items with their digital counterparts.

Additionally, it aims to foster and maintain an inclusive ecosystem around implementing partners.

Open Source

All protocols are based on modern cryp- tographic primitives and can be audited publicly due to the open source nature of the organization. The security and future proof of the protocols is an integral value of the OMBF that aims to develop long term established trust in the protocols as well as the implementations.

Interoperability

All protocols are maintained in an open source manner and can be contributed to by community members. The entire orga- nization operates in a non-profit manner. All work is published and meetings are open for anyone to join.

Security

It lies at the heart of the OMBF to create agnostic protocols which, in theory, can work with any smart contract platform. The implementation of the protocols is not limited to any specific type of distributed ledger or blockchain and could even be implemented with a centralized database.
